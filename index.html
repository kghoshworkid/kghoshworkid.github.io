About

Kunal Ghosh is a Senior Manager, Product Management II at Samsung Research America (Knox Research), with nearly a decade of experience in cybersecurity. His work spans on-device threat defense — including phishing protection and dropper detection — identity and access management (IAM), and biometric authentication for consumer devices. He is the named owner of shipped OEM security features, having delivered capabilities such as device compliance sharing with identity providers (IdPs) and Universal Logout. He leads technical tracks across Samsung Account for Business, on-device threat detection, and enterprise identity. His current interests extend to AI governance and agentic identity — how access-control and trust frameworks evolve for autonomous, AI-driven systems.

Selected work

  • Device compliance sharing with identity providers Signals device posture to IdPs so access decisions can factor real-time compliance — bridging on-device security state and enterprise identity.
  • Universal Logout Coordinated session termination across services, aligned with the Shared Signals / CAEP direction for revoking access when risk changes.
  • On-device threat defense — phishing & dropper detection OEM-integrated protection that detects phishing and dropper behavior locally on the device, without offloading user data.
